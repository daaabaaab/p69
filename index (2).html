Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

The church is a holy grail to all our students. We read a verse from the bible everyday in the morning before starting school. In our school, we believe Jesus is the true god.



school library

Was built in 1980

At our library, we allow all students to come at break time to read a book in the quiet library and be able to borrow books.





school auditorium

Was built in 1982

The auditorium is our school assembly and event room. All the students sit in their allocated position in the auditorium and listen respectfully to the people on stage.
